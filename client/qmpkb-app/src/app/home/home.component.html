Mouse Quantitative Proteomics Knowledgebase - MouseQuaPro

Mus Musculus is the most common animal model in research. To facilitate in-depth discovery and drug testing as well as development, the UVIC Proteomics Centre strives to develop and validate quantitative proteomics assays for mouse proteins. Currently MouseQuaPro lists validated assays for surrogate peptides that can be used to quantify mouse proteins. of these assays correspond to human proteins.

In addition to assays detailed information, the knowledge base includes reference protein concentration ranges in various tissues generated by using these assays.
